About

Hi there, Welcome!

I created this website based on a challenge from a class I’m taking through Skillcrush. We we’re given the option of creating a website about retreats that was already defined or creating a website from scratch about something we were passionate about. I’m passionate about awareness regarding Mixed Expressive Receptive Language Disorder (MERLD). I have a high schooler who was diagnosed with it in second grade, along with a reading and writing disorder. The road has been tough and I wish there was more information out there. My hope in creating this website was to try to gather information in one place (oh, and also to develop mad coding skillz ). I did a lot of googling to find information and this website is by no means exhaustive. It is just a start.

Take a look at the sweet video on this page created by a family impacted by MERLD. -- Carol
